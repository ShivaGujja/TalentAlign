Job Title: Technical Solutions Architect – AI & Cloud
Location: Hyderabad, India

Define and create technical roadmaps aligned with business objectives.

Architect cloud-native, microservices-based solutions with a focus on scalability, resilience, and performance.

Design and implement DevSecOps architectures to ensure secure and automated delivery pipelines.

Lead the design and adoption of MLOps frameworks for AI/ML deployment and lifecycle management.

Architect LLM/AI-based solutions, integrating emerging AI technologies into enterprise systems.

Guide data engineering architectures, including data pipelines, lakes, and real-time processing.

Partner with business stakeholders, product owners, and engineering teams to align solutions with client requirements.

Provide leadership in technology selection, PoCs, and best practice adoption.

Mentor technical teams and ensure adherence to architectural standards.

Required Skills & Qualifications
Bachelor’s/Master’s degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or related field.

10+ years of experience in software engineering, with 3+ years in a solutions/technical architect role.

Proven expertise in cloud platforms (AWS, Azure, or GCP).

Strong understanding of microservices, APIs, event-driven architectures, and security best practices.

Hands-on experience with DevOps/DevSecOps tools (Docker, Kubernetes, Jenkins, CI/CD).

Knowledge of MLOps, AI/LLM integration, and Data Engineering frameworks.

Strong proficiency with databases (relational and NoSQL).

Excellent communication, presentation, and stakeholder management skills.
